Vibration Guide

Technical Manual for Energy Awareness & Manipulation

What Vibration Is (Actually)

Everything vibrates. Not metaphorically — literally. Atoms vibrate. Light vibrates. Thoughts vibrate. Emotions vibrate. The difference between you and a stone is vibrational rate.

Hermeticism teaches: you can change your vibration deliberately. This is the core skill. Raise vibration = clarity, energy, manifestation. Lower vibration = grounding, stability, rest.

The Vibration Scale (1-10)

Learn this scale. Check your vibration multiple times daily. Within 2 weeks, you'll feel it instantly.

1 Dead/Numb
2 Depressed/Heavy
3 Apathetic/Tired
4 Fearful/Anxious
5 Neutral/Normal
6 Calm/Focused
7 Energetic/Clear
8 Joyful/Radiant
9 Ecstatic/Unified
10 Divine/Gnosis

How to Use This Scale

  • Morning check: What's my vibration on waking? (Records baseline)
  • Pre-practice: What is it before meditation/ritual? (Measures starting point)
  • Post-practice: What is it after? (Measures effectiveness)
  • Random checks: 3-5x daily, just ask "What number am I?" (Builds awareness)
  • Trigger tracking: What raises you? What lowers you? (Identifies patterns)

Feeling Vibration (Calibration)

Most people can't feel vibration at first. You've been trained to ignore it. This recalibrates your sensitivity.

Exercise 1: Hand Sensitivity

  1. Rub your hands together vigorously for 30 seconds
  2. Stop. Hold palms facing each other, 6 inches apart
  3. Slowly move them closer, then farther (like an accordion)
  4. Feel the resistance? The warmth? The tingling? That's vibration.
  5. Close your eyes. Focus only on the sensation between your palms.
  6. Continue for 2-3 minutes.

Practice: 3x daily for 1 week. By day 7, you'll feel it instantly.

Exercise 2: Body Scan

  1. Sit or lie comfortably. Close eyes.
  2. Bring attention to your left foot. Feel any sensation (tingling, warmth, pressure, pulsing)
  3. Move to left ankle, calf, knee, thigh. Scan each area.
  4. Continue up through torso, arms, neck, head.
  5. Do not judge sensations. Just feel them.
  6. These are not "just nerve signals." This is energy. This is vibration.

Time: 10-15 minutes. Practice daily for 2 weeks.

Exercise 3: Environmental Vibration

  1. Enter a room. Stop. Close eyes for 10 seconds.
  2. Ask: "What is the vibration of this space?"
  3. Feel it: dense, light, agitated, calm, heavy, clear
  4. Open eyes. Note what correlates (lighting, clutter, people, electronics)
  5. Leave the room. Return. Does it feel different?

Practice: Do this in every space you enter for 1 week. You'll become a vibration detector.

Raising Vibration (Deliberate Shift)

Once you can feel vibration, you can change it. These are the most effective techniques (tested, not theoretical).

Method 1: Breath (Fastest)

  1. Sit upright. Spine straight.
  2. Breathe rapidly through your nose (1-2 seconds per breath)
  3. Make it deep — belly, not chest
  4. Continue for 2-3 minutes (60-90 breaths)
  5. Stop. Feel the shift. Vibration will be 2-4 points higher.

Use when: You need a quick shift (before work, before social, before practice)

Method 2: Light Visualization (Most Powerful)

  1. Close eyes. Enter meditation posture.
  2. Visualize brilliant white light above your head
  3. On each inhale, draw the light down through your body
  4. On each exhale, release darkness/density through your feet
  5. Continue for 10-20 minutes
  6. By the end, you will be glowing (others can feel it)

Use when: Deep practice, before ritual, after low-vibration exposure

Method 3: Sound/Vibration (Easiest)

  1. Put on high-vibration music (classical, binaural beats, kirtan, certain electronic)
  2. Or: chant a mantra (OM, EHEIEH, I AM)
  3. Or: use a singing bowl, tuning fork, or gong
  4. Let the sound vibrate through your body
  5. Feel your vibration synchronize with the sound

Use when: You're too tired for active practice. Sound does the work for you.

Method 4: Affirmation/Identity (Most Underrated)

  1. Stand in front of a mirror. Look yourself in the eyes.
  2. Say (out loud): "I am light. I am clear. I am powerful."
  3. Say it like you mean it. Not hopeful — declarative.
  4. Repeat 10-20 times
  5. Feel the shift. Your vibration will rise 1-3 points.

Why it works: Words are vibration. Spoken with intent, they change your field.

Lowering Vibration (Grounding)

Sometimes you need to come down. Too much high vibration = scattered, ungrounded, spacey. These bring you back to earth.

Method 1: Earth Connection

  1. Go outside. Stand barefoot on earth/grass/concrete (not wood, not carpet)
  2. Visualize roots growing from your feet into the earth
  3. Breathe slowly (4 counts in, 6 counts out)
  4. Feel yourself anchored, stable, heavy
  5. Continue for 5-10 minutes

Method 2: Heavy Food

  1. Eat something dense: root vegetables, meat, bread, salt
  2. Eat slowly. Feel the weight of it in your stomach.
  3. Affirm: "I am grounded. I am stable. I am here."

Use when: You're too "up" to function (can't sleep, can't focus, too energetic)

Method 3: Physical Exertion

  1. Exercise until you're tired (weights, running, yoga, sex)
  2. Physical exhaustion grounds you faster than anything
  3. Shower after. Feel the reset.

Reading Others' Vibrations

Once you're sensitive to your own vibration, you can feel others'. This is not psychic — it's energetic perception.

How to Feel Someone's Vibration

  1. Be in a calm state yourself (vibration 6-7)
  2. Look at the person (in person or photo)
  3. Ask: "What is their vibration?"
  4. Feel the first sensation that arises: dense, light, agitated, calm, warm, cold
  5. Assign a number (1-10)
  6. Check: does this match their behavior? (low vibe = tired/irritable, high vibe = radiant/engaged)

Practice: Do this with everyone you meet for 2 weeks. You'll become accurate.

Why This Matters

  • Protection: You'll feel toxic people before they drain you. Avoid or shield.
  • Alignment: You'll feel aligned people instantly. Seek them out.
  • Healing: You'll feel where others are blocked. You can help (if appropriate).
  • Manifestation: You'll feel when the universe is aligned with your intent. Act then.

Vibration & Manifestation

This is the secret: you manifest what you vibrate, not what you want.

Wanting money while vibrating lack = no money. Wanting love while vibrating loneliness = no love. Wanting health while vibrating sickness = no health.

Manifestation Protocol

  1. Clarify intent: What do you want? (Be specific)
  2. Check vibration: What number are you now?
  3. Raise to match: Use breath, light, sound to raise vibration to 8-9
  4. Feel it done: In high vibration, feel the desire as already fulfilled
  5. Release: Let go. The universe handles the how.
  6. Act: Take inspired action (you'll know what)

Key: Do not try to manifest from low vibration. It won't work. Raise first, then manifest.

⚠️ Common Mistake

People try to manifest from vibration 3-4 (lack, fear, desperation). This repels what they want.

Fix: Raise vibration first. Then manifest. Order matters.

Daily Vibration Practice

Morning (5 min)

  • Check vibration on waking (record in journal)
  • 3 minutes rapid breath (raise 2-3 points)
  • Set intention: "Today I maintain vibration 7+"

Midday (2 min)

  • Check vibration (just ask)
  • If below 6: 1 minute breath + affirmation
  • If 7+: maintain, don't interfere

Evening (10 min)

  • Check vibration
  • Light visualization (release the day's density)
  • Record in journal: what raised you, what lowered you

Weekly Review

  • What was your average vibration this week?
  • What were your highest moments? Recreate them.
  • What were your lowest moments? Avoid or reframe them.
  • Set intention for next week
