Ritual Guide

Banishing, Protection, and Reality Manipulation

⚠️ Read This First

Ritual is not magic thinking. Ritual is psychological technology. It programs your subconscious, focuses your intent, and shifts your vibration. Done properly, it changes reality — not through supernatural forces, but through you.

Do not skip the banishing. Do not rush. Do not perform when emotionally unstable.

🜂 The Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram (LBRP)

This is the foundation. Learn it. Practice it daily for at least 3 months before anything else. It clears your field, protects you, and prepares you for deeper work.

Preparation

  • Time: Same time daily (dawn or dusk ideal)
  • Space: Clean, quiet, private
  • Posture: Stand upright, feet shoulder-width
  • Direction: Face East to begin

Part 1: The Qabalistic Cross

  1. Touch forehead. Vibrate: ATEH (ah-teh)
  2. Touch groin. Vibrate: MALKUTH (mal-kooth)
  3. Touch right shoulder. Vibrate: VE-GEBURAH (veh-gev-oo-rah)
  4. Touch left shoulder. Vibrate: VE-GEDULAH (veh-geh-doo-lah)
  5. Clasp hands at chest. Vibrate: LE-OLAM AMEN (leh-oh-lam ah-men)

Visualize: A cross of light running through your body, anchoring you between heaven and earth.

Part 2: The Pentagrams

  1. East: Draw a blue pentagram in the air (start at hip, up to forehead, down to opposite hip, up to shoulder, across to shoulder, back to start). Thrust fingers into center. Vibrate: YHVH (yod-heh-vaw-heh)
  2. Turn to South. Draw pentagram. Vibrate: ADONAI (ah-do-nie)
  3. Turn to West. Draw pentagram. Vibrate: EHEIEH (eh-hey-yeh)
  4. Turn to North. Draw pentagram. Vibrate: AGLA (ah-glah)

Visualize: Each pentagram blazing with blue fire. You are inside a protective sphere.

Part 3: The Archangels

  1. Extend arms in cross. Say: "Before me, Raphael" (visualize blue light in front)
  2. "Behind me, Gabriel" (visualize red light behind)
  3. "On my right hand, Michael" (visualize yellow light on right)
  4. "On my left hand, Uriel" (visualize green/purple on left)
  5. "For about me flames the Pentagram. And in the column shines the six-rayed star."

Part 4: Closing

  1. Repeat the Qabalistic Cross (same as Part 1)
  2. Sit in silence for 2-5 minutes
  3. Feel the cleared, protected space

⚠️ Critical Notes

  • Vibration matters: Do not just say the words — vibrate them from your diaphragm. Feel them resonate.
  • Visualization matters: See the light. Feel the sphere. This is not imagination — it's energetic construction.
  • Daily practice: 3 months minimum. Then you can add other work.
  • Before any ritual: Always do LBRP first. It clears the space and protects you.

🜄 Protection & Shielding

You will encounter low-vibration people, places, and situations. These techniques protect your field.

Technique 1: The Egg of Light

  1. Stand or sit comfortably. Close eyes.
  2. Visualize white light above your head
  3. On each inhale, draw the light down around your body
  4. Let it form an egg-shaped sphere 2-3 feet around you
  5. Affirm: "Nothing harmful can enter this space. Only love, only light."
  6. Feel the sphere solidify. It is permeable to good, impermeable to harm.
  7. Continue for 5-10 minutes

Use when: Entering toxic environments, dealing with difficult people, before sleep

Technique 2: The Mirror Shield

  1. Visualize your egg of light (above)
  2. Now make the surface reflective — like a mirror
  3. Any negative energy sent toward you reflects back to its source
  4. Affirm: "I am protected. All harm returns to sender."
  5. Feel the mirror solidify

Use when: You know someone is sending negativity (intentionally or not)

Note: This is not attack. It's defense. The energy returns because you refuse to hold it.

Technique 3: Cutting Cords

  1. Sit quietly. Enter light meditation
  2. Visualize any person you feel entangled with
  3. See the cords/threads connecting you (they may be dark, thick, thin, many)
  4. Visualize a sword of white light in your hand
  5. Cut each cord. One by one. Feel them release
  6. Affirm: "I release you. You release me. We are free."
  7. Seal your field with the egg of light

Use when: Ending relationships, leaving jobs, detaching from toxic dynamics

🜁 Reality Manipulation (Manifestation)

This is the work. Not "law of attraction" fluff — actual reality manipulation through vibration, intent, and action.

Protocol 1: The Basic Manifestation Ritual

  1. Clear: Perform LBRP (clears your field)
  2. Raise: Use breath or light to raise vibration to 8-9
  3. Clarify: State your intent clearly: "I manifest [specific outcome] for the highest good of all"
  4. Feel: In high vibration, feel the outcome as already achieved. Not "I will have" — "I have."
  5. Release: Let go. The universe handles the how. Your job is the what.
  6. Act: Take inspired action within 48 hours (you'll know what)
  7. Trust: Do not check obsessively. Trust the process.

Time: 20-30 minutes. Perform once per intent. Do not repeat daily (this is desperation vibration).

Protocol 2: Sigil Work (Chaos/Hermetic Hybrid)

  1. Write intent: "I MANIFEST [OUTCOME]" (present tense, positive, specific)
  2. Remove repeats: Cross out duplicate letters. E.g., "I MANIFEST MONEY" → I M A N F E S T O Y
  3. Create symbol: Combine remaining letters into an abstract symbol
  4. Charge: Enter gnosis state (through breath, meditation, or exhaustion)
  5. Gaze: Stare at the sigil until your mind goes blank
  6. Release: Destroy the sigil (burn, tear, bury). Forget it.

Why it works: The sigil bypasses conscious doubt and programs the subconscious directly.

Protocol 3: Timeline Jumping

  1. Enter deep meditation (vibration 8-9)
  2. Visualize multiple timelines branching from you
  3. See the timeline where your desire is already fulfilled
  4. Feel the difference: how does that version of you walk, talk, breathe?
  5. Step into that timeline. Feel the shift.
  6. Affirm: "I am now in the timeline where [outcome] is true."
  7. Live from that state. Do not return to the old timeline.

Warning: This is advanced. Do not attempt until you can reliably reach vibration 8+.

⚠️ Manifestation Ethics

  • Do not manipulate free will: "I manifest [specific person] loves me" = wrong. "I manifest a loving relationship" = right.
  • Do not harm: "I manifest [person] suffers" = karmic poison. Do not do this.
  • For highest good: Always add "for the highest good of all" to your intents. The universe knows better than you.
  • Accept no: Sometimes the universe says no. Trust it. What you want is not always what you need.

🜃 The Elements (Practical Work)

The four elements are not just symbols. They are actual energetic states. Master each one.

🜂 🜄 🜁 🜃

Fire (🜂) — Energy, Will, Transformation

  • Quality: Heat, movement, intensity, drive
  • When to use: Need energy, motivation, to break through resistance
  • Practice: Visualize red/orange fire in your belly. Feel it spread through your body. Move quickly. Act decisively.
  • Warning: Too much fire = burnout, anger, impulsiveness

Water (🜄) — Emotion, Intuition, Flow

  • Quality: Fluidity, depth, feeling, receptivity
  • When to use: Need emotional healing, intuitive insight, to go with the flow
  • Practice: Visualize blue water flowing through you. Feel your emotions move like water. Do not resist.
  • Warning: Too much water = overwhelm, moodiness, lack of boundaries

Air (🜁) — Mind, Communication, Clarity

  • Quality: Lightness, thought, speech, perspective
  • When to use: Need mental clarity, to communicate, to see the big picture
  • Practice: Visualize white/gold light air filling your head and chest. Feel your thoughts become clear. Speak with precision.
  • Warning: Too much air = dissociation, overthinking, lack of grounding

Earth (🜃) — Body, Stability, Manifestation

  • Quality: Solidity, grounding, patience, results
  • When to use: Need to ground, to manifest physically, to be patient
  • Practice: Visualize green/brown earth energy rising from the ground through your feet. Feel rooted. Feel stable.
  • Warning: Too much earth = stagnation, rigidity, resistance to change

Balance Practice

  1. Sit quietly. Enter meditation
  2. Invoke each element in turn: Fire (belly), Water (chest), Air (head), Earth (feet)
  3. Feel them all present, balanced, harmonized
  4. Affirm: "I am balanced. I am whole. I am complete."
  5. Rest in this state for 10-20 minutes

Practice: Daily for 2 weeks. Then as needed.

📅 Daily Practice Schedule

Months 1-3: Foundation

  • Morning: LBRP (10-15 min)
  • Evening: Journal (5 min)

Months 4-6: Building

  • Morning: LBRP + Meditation (25-35 min)
  • Evening: Vibration check + Journal (10 min)

Months 7-12: Advanced

  • Morning: LBRP + Meditation + Element work (40-50 min)
  • Evening: Manifestation or Sigil work (as needed)
  • Weekly: Full ritual (LBRP + Protection + Manifestation)
