Study Syllabus

12-month Hermetic path with Masonic preparation

Important: This is a marathon, not a sprint. Rushing = superficial understanding. Let each phase settle before moving to the next. Your neurodivergence is an advantage here — deep focus, pattern recognition. Use it.

☤ Phase 1: Foundation

Months 1-3

Reading

  • The Emerald Tablet — Memorise all 14 verses. Meditate on one verse per day.
  • The Kybalion — One principle per week for 7 weeks. Practice each actively.
  • Corpus Hermeticum (Copenhaver) — Books I, IV, IX, XIII to start. Read slowly, with commentary.
  • The Stoic Hermetic Path (your existing file) — Practical daily application.

Practice

  • Meditation: 10 min daily → build to 30 min by month 3
  • Breath work: Rhythmic breathing, 5 min daily
  • Journal: Record dreams, synchronicities, observations
  • Vibration tracking: Check 3x daily (1-10 scale)

Milestone (End of Month 3)

✅ Checkpoint

  • Emerald Tablet memorised
  • 7 Principles understood and practiced
  • Meditation 30 min daily, consistent
  • Vibration awareness developed (can feel shifts)
  • 30+ journal entries

🜂 Phase 2: Practice Begins

Months 4-6

Reading

  • Initiation Into Hermetics (Bardon) — Steps 1-5. This is the core work now.
  • The Middle Pillar (Regardie) — Energy circulation techniques
  • Liber O (Crowley) — Pentagram ritual, vibration work. Start here for Crowley.
  • Liber Resh (Crowley) — Solar adoration, 4x daily. Simple, practical, powerful.

Practice

  • IIH Step 1: Thought control (15 min daily)
  • IIH Step 2: Dominance of thoughts (10 min daily)
  • IIH Step 3: Mental emptiness (15 min daily)
  • IIH Step 4: Observing thoughts (15 min daily)
  • Breath work: Rhythmic + pore breathing (15 min daily)
  • Middle Pillar: Full circulation (15 min daily)
  • Vibration work: Learn to feel and shift deliberately

Milestone (End of Month 6)

✅ Checkpoint

  • IIH Steps 1-5 complete
  • Can hold mental emptiness for 15+ min
  • Vibration shift is deliberate, not accidental
  • Middle Pillar circulation felt clearly
  • 60+ journal entries with results tracking

🏛️ Phase 3: Masonic Preparation

Months 7-9

Reading

  • The Meaning of Masonry (Wilmshurst) — Best single book on Masonic philosophy
  • The Secret Doctrine of Freemasonry (Manly P. Hall) — Esoteric angle
  • Morals and Dogma (Albert Pike) — Selected chapters (Kabbalah, symbolism)
  • From the Quarry (YouTube) — Continue watching, now with context

Research

  • Visit 3-5 London lodges (open evenings)
  • Speak to recent initiates about their experience
  • Compare UGLE vs. irregular lodges (Co-Masonry, etc.)
  • Understand costs, time commitments, progression

Practice (Continue)

  • IIH Steps 6-8
  • Daily meditation + breath work (non-negotiable)
  • Reality testing: small manifestations weekly

Milestone (End of Month 9)

✅ Checkpoint

  • 3+ lodges visited, notes written
  • Decision made: which lodge to petition (or not)
  • Masonic philosophy understood (not just history)
  • IIH Steps 6-8 complete
  • 90+ journal entries

☾ Phase 4: Deep Work

Months 10-12

Reading

  • Corpus Hermeticum — Complete remaining books
  • The Chymical Wedding of Christian Rosenkreutz — Rosicrucian text
  • Asclepius — Companion to Corpus Hermeticum
  • Crowley Deep Dive (see below) — If ready for heavier Thelemic work
  • Lodge-specific materials (if you've joined)

Practice

  • IIH Steps 9-10
  • Liber O daily (pentagram ritual)
  • Advanced vibration work: feel others' vibrations
  • Reality manipulation: deliberate manifestation practice
  • Teaching: explain concepts to others (solidifies understanding)

Milestone (End of Month 12)

✅ Checkpoint

  • IIH Steps 1-10 complete (foundation done)
  • Vibration work: can read others, shift self instantly
  • Manifestation: consistent small results, building to larger
  • 120+ journal entries (full year documented)
  • Masonic decision made and acted on
  • Ready for Phase 5: Specialisation

🜚 Phase 5: Specialisation (Year 2+)

Ongoing

Paths to Consider

  • Ceremonial Magic: Golden Dawn system, Enochian, etc.
  • Kabbalah: Tree of Life, pathworking, divine names
  • Alchemy: Inner (consciousness) and outer (laboratory)
  • Masonic High Degrees: Scottish Rite, York Rite, research lodges
  • Teaching: Guide others (only after mastery)

Crowley Reading Path (if ready)

📚 Progressive study order

  1. Liber O — Already done in Phase 2. Foundation.
  2. Liber Resh — Already done in Phase 2. Daily practice.
  3. Little Essays Toward Truth — Short, sharp, practical. Read one per day.
  4. The Law is for All — Commentary on Book of the Law. Context first.
  5. Magick in Theory and Practice — Part III (theory), then rituals.
  6. The Book of the Law (Liber AL) — Read with commentary, not alone.
  7. The Confessions — Autobiography. Take with salt — he mythmade himself.
  8. Book of Thoth — Tarot symbolism, if that's your path.

Warning: Don't start with Book of the Law alone — it's cryptic without context. Work through Liber O and Resh first (already in Phase 2), then build up.

Remember

  • The path is infinite. There is no "done."
  • Integration > accumulation. Live it, don't just read it.
  • Your neurodivergence is an asset. Pattern recognition, deep focus, systems thinking.
  • Balance: spiritual work, physical health, relationships, practical life.
